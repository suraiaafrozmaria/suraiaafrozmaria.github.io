Remotely Operated Underwater Vehicle
This project is a part of my undergraduate thesis. The Remotely Operated Underwater Vehicle (ROUV) is designed with a unique 3D design providing six degrees of freedom. The project also includes a unique hardware implementation, image denoising, and object detection. The ROUV is specifically designed for exploration, search, and rescue missions under the water, maintaining fluid dynamics.
